About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Computational Vision and Robotics Laboratory (CVRL) at FORTH, affiliated with the University of Crete. My research focuses on combining structured knowledge with data-driven approaches in artificial intelligence, with particular emphasis on hybrid AI systems, knowledge graphs, and computer vision applications.

I completed my PhD in Computer Science at the University of Crete in 2025, where I worked on combining structured knowledge and data-driven representations for object states classification under the supervision of Professor Dimitris Plexousakis and Professor Antonis Argyros.
